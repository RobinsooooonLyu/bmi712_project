Main diagnosis/diagnoses:
Peripheral bronchopulmonary, histologically mixed adenocarcinoma of the right upper lobe of
the lung with a very predominant large-cell and partly clear-cell, small acinar component,
focal mucin formation and bronchioloalveolar growth pattern at the tumor margin and
localization in S2.
TNM classification on the basis of this material pT2 pN1 pMX RX, stage IIB.
C34.1; M 8255/3.

Comment/supplementary remark:
The carcinoma shows infiltration and circumscribed perforation of the visceral pleura, which
explains the RX category. It has also produced a hilar lymph node metastasis. The resection
margin of the bronchus and vessels, the resection material from the middle lobe, the piece of
parietal pleura and the lymph nodes of samples 4 to 13 are tumor-free.
