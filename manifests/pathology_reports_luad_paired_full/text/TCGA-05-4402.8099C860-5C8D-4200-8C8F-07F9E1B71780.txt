Main diagnosis/diagnoses:
Mixed adenocarcinoma located peripherally in the lower lobe with a predominant acinar
component, a smaller large-cell solid component with a bronchioloalveolar growth pattern in
the tumor periphery, slight mucin formation and marked lymph vessel propagation in the lung
tissue and the visceral pleura.

Comment/supplementary remark:
Conventional histology shows a peripheral bronchopulmonary carcinoma (to be verified by
immunohistological analysis). The tumor has infiltrated the visceral pleura, focally penetrated
the surface of the pleura and extends as far as the basal margin of the preparation after the row
of metal clamps is removed.
These findings thus give a tumor classification of pT2 pNX pMX L1 RX, with localization
code C34.3 and morphology code M8255/3.
